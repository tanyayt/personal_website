# Online Books

[Dive into Deep Learning](https://d2l.ai): A comprehensive book that introduces deep learning in an approachable way, with the concepts, context and the code

[Deep Learning](https://www.deeplearningbook.org/) by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio and Aaron Courville, "is a resource intended to help students and practitioners enter the field of machine learning in general and deep learning in particular". 

[Deep Learning for Coders with Fastai and Pytorch: AI Applications Without a PhD](https://github.com/fastai/fastbook) by Howard, J & Gugger, S.  covers deep learning, fastai, PyTorch and used in an MOOC course (https://course.fast.ai)
